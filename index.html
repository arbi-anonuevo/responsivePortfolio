About Me

Hello! My name is Ron Anonuevo, but I commonly go by Arbi. I'm a Rutgers University alumni with a B.S. in Exercise Science - Kinesiology.

MY EXPERIENCE

I have 8 years of experience as a personal trainer where my job was to help individuals achieve their health/fitness goals by analyzing their movement patterns, dietary intake and general lifestyle habits. My time as a one-on-one personal trainer is where I learned how to properly communicate with my peers/clients so that I could ensure they had a deep understanding on how to properly lose weight or rehabilitate joints in a structured fashion.

Eventually I transitioned into group fitness where I learned how to work with large groups so I could safely and effectively teach classes of 4-20 individuals per session.


WHERE I'M GOING

Due to COVID-19 I had to make a career change. The instability of working in the fitness space, especially in the middle of a pandemic, created huge uncertainty about my future. I always had an interest in learning software development but never had the time nor the right instructor, so here I am working to become a strong Software Developer to join the work force!
